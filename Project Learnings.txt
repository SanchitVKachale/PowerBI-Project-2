Project Learnings:-

1)Created interactive dashboard to track and analyze online sales data.

2)Used complex parameters to drill down in worksheet and customization using filters and slicers.

3)Created connections, joined new tables, calculations to manipulate data and enable user driven parameters for visualizations.

4)Used different type of customized visualizations(bar chart, pie chart, donut chart, clustered bar chart, scatter chart, line chart, area chart, map, slicers)

Insights:-

Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh have the highest amount of sales in the Clothing, Electronics and Furniture categories respectively.

Cash On Delivery(COD) is the most used payment method followed by Unified Payments Interface(UPI) and Debit Card.

Printers, Bookcases and Sarees are the most Profit generating Sub-categories.
